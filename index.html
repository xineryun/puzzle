How to Play

Simple rules, deep strategy

  1. 1

    Swipe up, down, left, or right to move number tiles. Identical tiles merge into one (2+2=4).

  2. 2

    Each move spawns a new tile (2 or 4). Plan carefully to avoid running out of space.

  3. 3

    Special rules: On larger boards (5x5, 6x6), chain merges can happen across rows/columns.

  4. 4

    Levels: Beginner (4x4 → 2048), Intermediate (5x5 → 4096), Expert (6x6 → 8192).

  5. 5

    Win by reaching the target number. Lose if no moves or merges remain.
